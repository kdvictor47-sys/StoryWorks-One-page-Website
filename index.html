The challenge & our solution

Reducing back-and-forth between clients and Innovate By Day.

The problem

Clients arrive with strong ideas—but their project details are often scattered across emails, vague descriptions, or half-finished decks. This creates:

  • Multiple clarification emails about goals and deliverables
  • Misalignment on tone, visuals, and storytelling
  • Delays before production can even begin

For a storytelling-focused studio like Innovate By Day, unclear briefs mean teams spend precious time translating raw thoughts into structured instructions—instead of focusing on strategy and creative execution.

Our solution

StoryToDesign Spec is an AI tool embedded on Innovate By Day’s homepage. It guides clients through an adaptive form, then turns their answers into a structured, ready-to-review creative brief.

  • Guided questions uncover objectives, audience and storytelling goals
  • Language suggestions align client wording with Innovate By Day’s brand values
  • Generated brief becomes a shared source of truth for both sides

Key benefits for Innovate By Day & clients

Less back-and-forth

The adaptive form asks the right follow-up questions early, so teams aren’t chasing missing information later.

On-brand from day one

AI suggests ways to refine client wording so project briefs stay coherent with Innovate By Day’s brand voice and storytelling approach.

Faster project kickoff

The generated creative brief can be reviewed, edited, and approved quickly—unlocking production sooner.

From rough idea to clear brief in a few steps

1

Client shares their story

They enter their campaign idea, goals and any initial thoughts into the StoryToDesign Spec form.

2

AI asks smarter follow-ups

The form adapts, generating questions about audience, channels, tone and deliverables.

3

Creative brief is generated

A structured brief is produced for the client to review and for the Innovate By Day team to refine.

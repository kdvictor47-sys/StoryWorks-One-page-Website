Features that turn stories into specs

StoryToDesign Spec enhances Innovate By Day’s service delivery by transforming client inputs into structured project briefs that stay true to campaign goals and brand identity.

Core capabilities

Adaptive AI form

The form responds to each client’s inputs by generating tailored follow-up questions about objectives, audiences, brand voice, storytelling goals and deliverables.

Brand-aligned language

As clients type, AI offers suggestions to refine their wording so it better matches Innovate By Day’s tone and core values, while preserving their intent.

Document-aware context

Clients can upload brand guidelines, previous campaigns or scripts. The AI uses these documents to shape more accurate and relevant briefs.

Structured creative brief

The system outputs a clean creative brief including project overview, objectives, audience, key messages, tone, deliverables and timelines.

Iterative refinement

When new information appears, clients can add supplementary inputs instead of restarting the form. The brief updates as a living document.

Internal & client-friendly

The generated brief is easy to share internally at Innovate By Day and externally with client stakeholders for quick alignment.
